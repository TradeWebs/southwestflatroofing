GRP Fibreglass Roofing

A seamless, joint-free fibreglass roof that's built to last. GRP is our go-to recommendation for extensions, garages, balconies and bay windows.

Call Now - 07477 140732 Get a free quote

GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) fibreglass roofing is one of the toughest flat roofing options available. Because it's laid as a liquid and cures into a single seamless membrane, there are no joints or overlaps for water to find its way in through, which makes it a strong choice for roofs that see a lot of foot traffic or sit under trees.

We use GRP most often on house extensions, garages, dormers, balconies and bay window roofs, where a hard-wearing, low-maintenance finish matters. It's available in a range of colours to match the rest of your property, and it comes with our 10-year guarantee as standard.

Every GRP job starts with a free site survey, so we can check the existing roof structure and give you an accurate quote before any work begins.
