EPDM Rubber Roofing

A single sheet of hard-wearing rubber membrane, laid in one piece with minimal joints. Quick to install and built to handle the British weather for decades.

Call Now - 07477 140732 Get a free quote

EPDM is a synthetic rubber roofing membrane that's been used on UK flat roofs for decades, and for good reason. It's flexible, resistant to UV damage and extreme temperatures, and because it's usually laid in a single large sheet, there's very little chance of it failing at a seam.

It's a great option where speed of installation matters, or where the roof shape doesn't suit fibreglass as well. Like all our flat roofing work, it's backed by a 10-year guarantee, and if it's ever damaged, EPDM is straightforward to patch-repair without needing to replace the whole roof.

Get in touch for a free survey and we'll tell you honestly whether EPDM or GRP is the better fit for your roof.
