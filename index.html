What we do

Not sure what your roof needs?
That's our job, not yours.

Every flat roof is different - we'll survey yours free of charge and recommend the right material for the job, not the most expensive one.

GRP fibreglass roofing

A seamless fibreglass finish with no joints and nowhere for water to get in. The toughest option for house extensions, garages, balconies and bay windows.

EPDM rubber roofing

A single sheet of hard-wearing rubber, laid in one piece. Quick to fit, handles the British weather for decades, and easy to repair if it's ever damaged.

Felt roof repairs

Got an older felt roof that's leaking or blistering? We trace the leak and repair it properly - or tell you honestly if it's time for a re-cover.

Fascias, soffits & UPVC cladding

Rotten fascia boards let water into your roofline. We replace fascias, soffits and guttering, and fit low-maintenance UPVC cladding.

Building services

More than roofs - extensions, structural work and general building, managed by the same team from start to finish.

Damp proofing

Specialist damp proofing to stop moisture problems at the source - not just cover them up.

Common questions

Straight answers.

What types of flat roofing do you specialise in?

GRP (fibreglass), EPDM (rubber) and felt flat roofing - installation, replacement and repair, for both homes and commercial buildings.

How long does a flat roof installation take?

It depends on the size of the roof and the complexity of the job. Most domestic flat roofs are done in a few days - we'll give you a clear timeline with your free quote.

Do you offer flat roof maintenance and repairs?

Yes - we repair and maintain flat roofs as well as installing them, including patch repairs, leak tracing and full re-covers.

Can I see examples of your previous work?
